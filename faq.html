How to Use the Search Feature on SPYshow?

1. Search by Movie Name:
To quickly find a specific movie, simply type the title in the search bar. For example, if you're looking for Terrifier 3, just type "Terr" and the system will display matching movies, including Terrifier 3.

2. Filter by Genre:
Use the filter options to narrow down your movie selection. You can choose from a variety of genres, such as Horror, Action, Comedy, and more. This helps you quickly discover films in your preferred category.

3. Partial Title Search:
If you're unsure of the full title, you can type the first few letters. For example, typing "Harr" will show all movies starting with "Harr" (such as Harry Potter). This is especially useful for finding movies with long or complicated names.

4. Advanced Search (Optional):
For more specific results, you can combine filters like genre, release year, and even language. This ensures you find exactly what you're looking for with minimal effort.
